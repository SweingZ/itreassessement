#3 MR ROBOT

INFO

Elliot, a brilliant but highly unstable young cyber-security engineer and vigilante hacker, becomes a key figure in a complex game of global dominance when he and his shadowy allies try to take down the corrupt organization he works for.
        Created by                                               Sam Esmail
        Written by                                                Sam Esmail, Randolph Leon

        Cinematography                                  Tod Campbell

        Year                                                              2015-2019
        IMDB Rating                                            8.6/10
        Genre                                                          Crime, Drama, Thriller
        Seasons                                                     4 Seasons
CAST
Rami Malek
Carly Chaikin
Christian Slater
Portia Doubleday

WHY I LOVE IT

One of the most creative, unique and original shows that i've ever watched. The writing is fantastic, the acting is extremely good and the music is so fitting for the world it takes pace in. Mr robot is a show with many twists, all performed brilliantly.

This show is insanely dark at times, so be prepared. It manages to transend reality while also being one of the most realistic shows out there. The color palette is one of my favourite aspects of the show. Some episodes are quite slow, BUT season 4 is the greatest final seasons of TV that i've ever watched and I DO NOT say that lightly.

Pease, watch this show!!! You honestly dont need to know anything about it before watching. You dont have to be into computers or hacking. Just watch.

Verdict

       93
OUT OF 100
