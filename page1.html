WHY I LOVE IT

What can i say? Breaking bad is breaking bad. For lots of people this is the greatest show of all time. I share that opinion too. This show is a masterpiece. There's pretty much 0 flaws. A perfect show with perfect acting and a perfect story with a perfect script along with perfect cinematography.


It's hard for me to put this show into words. I'll start with Bryan Cranston and Aaron Paul. They're the heart of the show. The greatest acting duo i've ever seen I rarely say that a performance is perfect, but both Cranston and Paul perfected the performences of their characters.


Season 4 & 5 are both perfect seasons of television. Each season of the show gets better, and better, and better. That's not something that every show can do. If you need to watch a show and for some reason haven't watched Breaking Bad, then you should go watch it. It's not something you'll regret. Get through it and then let me know your thoughts.

Truly a legendary show
